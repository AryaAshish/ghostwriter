Epic 1: Instagram Page for Brand & Acquisition

Goal: Build early audience & warm up creator market.

User Stories

Story 1.1: Instagram Page Creation
As a founder
I want to create an Instagram page targeting Indian creators
So that we can start building awareness and credibility in the creator market

Acceptance Criteria:
Instagram business account created with professional branding
Bio clearly communicates value proposition for Indian creators
Contact information and website link included
Initial content strategy defined

Priority: High
Effort: 2 days

Story 1.2: Daily Content Publishing
As a marketer
I want to post 1–2 daily carousels/reels showcasing trending script examples
So that we can demonstrate our AI's capability and attract potential users

Acceptance Criteria:
Content calendar with 30 days of posts planned
Mix of carousels and reels showcasing different niches
Trending script examples that resonate with Indian creators
Consistent posting schedule established

Priority: High
Effort: Ongoing (1-2 hours daily)

Story 1.3: Engagement Tracking
As a team member
I want to track engagement and DMs
So that we can identify potential beta users and measure market interest

Acceptance Criteria:
Analytics dashboard to track likes, comments, shares, saves
DM monitoring system for lead capture
Weekly engagement reports
Beta user identification process

Priority: Medium
Effort: 3 days

Story 1.4: Viral Content Strategy
As a growth hacker
I want to test creator meme-style posts and "If XYZ explained AI" content
So that we can maximize reach and virality among our target audience

Acceptance Criteria:
10 different meme formats tested
"If XYZ explained AI" series launched
A/B testing framework for content performance
Viral content templates documented

Priority: Medium
Effort: 5 days

Success Metrics
Instagram followers: 1000+ in first month
Daily engagement rate: >5%
DMs from potential users: 50+ per week
Beta user signups from Instagram: 25+
